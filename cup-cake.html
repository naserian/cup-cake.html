What is a cup cake?

A cup cake is a small cake baked in a cup shaped container and typically iced.

Types of cup cakes

Cup cakes come in different types such as:

  • Dark chcolate cupcakes with peanut butter frosting
  • Guinness,Whiskey and Irish Cream Cupcakes
  • Homemade Funfetti cupcakes
  • Margaritta cupcakes
  • Mocha cupcakes with Espresso Frosting
  • Pumpkin Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting
  • Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes
Those are just a few of the types of cakes which are most preferred by a wide range of people.
